Giovanni Foppiani – Visual Designer & Researcher

About

Through branding, graphic, web, UX, and service design, I help brands, associations, institutions, and artists — which add value to the planet, people, and art — to strengthen their communication potential.

Along my design practice, I experimented with a large variety of media, from analogue to digital, from traditional printed forms to animated designs. In my opinion, each medium is a design object that must emphasize its unique characteristics.

Over the past years, I had the chance to travel and work in different European countries. Collaborating with diverse professionals allowed me to explore how impactful design can be for humans, society, and the planet.
