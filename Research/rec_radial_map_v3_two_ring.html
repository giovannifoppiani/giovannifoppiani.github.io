Designing regenerating energy commons

Giovanni Foppiani · DC 10 · Energy Mobility Systems

The Regenerative Energy Commons are purposeful and intertwined ecosystems weaving relationships among human and non-human actors to actively enhance aliveness toward net positive outcomes for planetary and societal wellbeing through poly-centric and co-managed energy system configurations.

PhD research

This research operates from a Design Science standpoint, conceiving energy systems at the nexus of socio-technical transitions (Geels) and socio-ecological systems (Ostrom) — proposing them as commons platforms where energy functions as social currency.
