About

Through branding, graphic, web, UX, and service design, I help brands, associations, institutions, and artists — which add value to the planet, people, and art — to strengthen their communication potential.

Along my design practice, I experimented with a variety of media, from analogue to digital ones. Each medium must emphasize its unique characteristics.

Over the past years, I had the chance to travel and collaborate with diverse professionals, exploring how impactful design can be for society and the planet.
