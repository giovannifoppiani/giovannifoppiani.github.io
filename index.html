Giovanni Foppiani

Visual Designer & researcher

Through my design practice, I collaborate with eco-social conscious brands, collectives, and associations of the third sector to strengthen their communication potential.

By investigating the mutual relationships between ecology, technology, and social ecosystems, my research leads to scenarios that frame new ways of conceiving interspecies cooperation and collaborations.
