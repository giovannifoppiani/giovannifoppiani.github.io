Giovanni Foppiani

visual designer & researcher

About

Through his design practice, he collaborates with eco-social conscious brands, public institution, and associations to strengthen their communication potential.

By investigating the mutual relationships between ecology, technology, and social ecosystems, his research leads to scenarios that frame new ways of conceiving interspecies cooperation and collaborations.

Bio

Giovanni Foppiani, designer and researcher at Università Iuav di Venezia, explores ecological and social interactions within techno-social ecosystems. His work focuses particularly on digital sustainability and social innovation. He collaborates with eco-socially conscious brands, collectives, and associations to enhance their communication capabilities.
